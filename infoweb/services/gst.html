GST Services

Expert GST compliance, advisory, and representation for growing businesses

Overview

Goods and Services Tax (GST) compliance is a crucial responsibility for every registered business in India. At N D SAMA, we provide end-to-end GST solutions designed to ensure accuracy, compliance, and complete peace of mind.

Our experienced Chartered Accountants assist businesses at every stage — from registration and return filing to audits and departmental representation — while offering strategic advisory support to minimize risks and penalties.

What We Do

  • GST Registration & Amendments – New registrations, amendments, cancellation, and migration assistance.
  • GST Return Filing – Timely filing of GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, GSTR-9, and other applicable returns.
  • GST Audit & Annual Compliance – Reconciliation and audit support to ensure accurate reporting.
  • GST Notices & Representation – Drafting replies, handling scrutiny, and departmental coordination.
  • GST Advisory & Planning – Classification guidance, input tax credit optimization, and compliance strategy.

Who Needs This Service?

  • Businesses registered or required to register under GST
  • Startups, SMEs, and growing enterprises
  • Manufacturers, traders, and wholesalers
  • Service providers across all industries
  • E-commerce sellers and exporters
